Welcome to Sitco

Welcome to Sitco Associates - Building the Future of Our Nation

Established in 2012, Sitco Associates is a renowned partnership firm and a leading construction company based in Kerala. Our registered office is located in Thrikkakara, Ernakulam District. Guided by the visionary Mr. M.P. Sidheek, we began our journey with the Cochin Refinery Project. Since then, our proven track record and unwavering commitment to quality have made us the first choice for major projects.

At Sitco, we don't just construct buildings; we realize dreams. With decades of experience, a relentless pursuit of excellence, and a spirit of innovation, we breathe life into your construction projects.

Our commitment to excellence is not just a statement; it's a way of doing business. Every project we undertake is a testament to our dedication to quality, timely completion, and client satisfaction

About Us

Founded by Mr. M.P. Sidheek in 1995, Sitco Associates embarked on its journey with a small team and a big vision. The Cochin Refinery Project marked the genesis of our commitment to quality. In 1995, a strategic decision led us to diversify our portfolio, taking on projects from various government bodies at both central and state levels. This marked a turning point, solidifying our position as a prominent construction company in Kerala, particularly excelling in government projects since 1995.

Over the years, Sitco Associates has garnered a reputation for delivering projects of exceptional quality, within budget, and on time. Our success story is built on a foundation of integrity, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to client satisfaction.
